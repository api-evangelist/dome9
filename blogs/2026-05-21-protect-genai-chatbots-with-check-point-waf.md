Generative AI chatbots are quickly becoming the new front door to your business. They answer customer questions, support employees, guide buyers, and increasingly connect to internal documents, APIs, knowledge bases, and business workflows. That convenience creates a new risk for you, chatbot may now speak on behalf of your brand, access sensitive information, or influence customer decisions.

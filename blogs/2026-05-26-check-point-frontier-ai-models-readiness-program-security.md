At Check Point we don’t wait for threats to evolve; we evolve ahead of them. This is why we’ve been running our Frontier AI Models Readiness Program: a proactive, structured initiative designed to ensure that our products remain resilient as AI models grow increasingly capable of understanding complex software systems and assisting adversaries in attacking them. As part of this program, we conducted large-scale AI-driven code scanning across our products, performed extensive security reviews, hardened components where needed, refined our time-to-patch procedures, and accelerated our protection
